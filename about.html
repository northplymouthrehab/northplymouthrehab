Our approach

Assessment, education and progressive exercise.

We do not believe in one-size-fits-all rehabilitation. Your plan should make sense to you, fit your life and progress at the right pace.

Listen first

We take time to understand your history, goals, concerns and what you want to get back to.

Explain clearly

You should leave knowing what we think is going on and what the next steps are.

Build capacity

Rehabilitation is progressed carefully so you feel stronger, more confident and prepared.
